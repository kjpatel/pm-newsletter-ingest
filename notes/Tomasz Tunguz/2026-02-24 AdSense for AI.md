## Summary

Koah, a new monetization platform for AI applications, has raised $20.5M Series A to build an advertising layer that contextually matches ads to specific moments in user conversations, achieving 4-5x higher click-through rates than traditional display advertising.

## Key Takeaways

- **Understand semantic context**: AI advertising works by analyzing full conversation context and user intent in real-time, not just keywords or user profiles, enabling ads matched to specific purchase decision moments.
- **Prioritize user experience**: Leading AI apps like Liner achieved 454% DAU growth while monetizing through Koah's native ad formats, maintaining near 100% retention by avoiding trust erosion.
- **Leverage the timing advantage**: Position relevant vendor solutions at critical decision points in agentic conversations (e.g., database selection for developers) where users actively seek recommendations.
- **Expect significant CTR improvements**: Koah's native formats demonstrate 7.5% average click-through rates and 122% higher CTR than previous display implementations, creating a new performance benchmark.
