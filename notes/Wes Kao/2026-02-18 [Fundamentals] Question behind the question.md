# [Fundamentals] Question behind the question

**Source**: [Wes Kao](https://newsletter.weskao.com/p/fundamentals-question-behind-the)
**Author**: Wes Kao | **Date**: Feb 18, 2026

---

## Summary

The 'question behind the question' (QBQ) is a fundamental communication skill that helps you understand the true underlying concern someone is asking about, rather than just answering the surface-level tactical question. By identifying and addressing what's really being asked, you can respond at the appropriate level of detail and build stronger professional relationships.

## Key Takeaways

- **Pause before answering** to identify the deeper concern behind surface-level questions—this takes only seconds once practiced but prevents repetitive follow-ups and clarifications.
- **Tailor your response altitude** based on who's asking: journalists want newsworthy soundbites, executives care about business impact, and customers may not fully articulate their root concern.
- **Use a soft probe script** like 'If you could share context on what's top of mind, it'll help me understand what's most relevant' instead of directly asking 'what's the question behind the question' which can sound aggressive.
- **Assert your interpretation and ask for correction** rather than directly challenging someone's question, since most people are happy to clarify if you've misunderstood their real concern.
