## Summary

AI-powered discovery transforms how PMs conduct user research by automating analysis workflows that traditionally took 10+ hours, making discovery feasible for every project rather than just critical ones. Caitlin Sullivan provides step-by-step processes for analyzing surveys and interviews with Claude to extract actionable insights with timestamped evidence.

## Key Takeaways

- **Automate interview analysis** using Claude Projects to process transcripts in 3-5 minutes per interview instead of 10+ hours, enabling discovery at scale across all projects
- **Structure your prompts** with clear context, desired outputs, evidence requirements, and actionability statements to generate usable insights instead of generic observations
- **Use timestamped evidence** from recordings and transcripts to back up findings so product decisions can be referenced and justified months later
- **Apply AI discovery to three use cases**: feature validation with user testing sessions, retention analysis through churned user interviews, and market research across hundreds of survey responses
- **Build discovery agents** in Claude Code that systematize your analysis workflow for consistent, repeatable research processes across your entire product team
