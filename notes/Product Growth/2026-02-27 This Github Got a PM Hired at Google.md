# This Github Got a PM Hired at Google

**Author**: Aakash Gupta | **Date**: Feb 27, 2026

## Summary

A GitHub profile is a powerful but underutilized tool for PM candidates that signals you actually build things and understand engineering workflows. This guide reveals how Shubham Saboo used GitHub to transition from Dev Rel to Senior AI PM at Google and provides a framework for other PMs to build standout profiles.

## Key Takeaways

- **Create a GitHub profile** to join the 24% of PM candidates who have one, as hiring managers will check it if present and it signals you actually ship products
- **Build tangible projects** that demonstrate version control, collaboration understanding, and daily engineering tool proficiency rather than just listing skills on a resume
- **Market your GitHub publicly** through posts and social media to turn it into an inbound lead magnet that attracts recruiter outreach, increasing your chances of interview-to-offer conversion
- **Focus on quality over scale** by creating meaningful projects that showcase PM skills—you don't need 76K stars to stand out, as candidates at OpenAI, Anthropic, and Meta AI were hired with modest repositories
- **Avoid GitHub as a resume liability** by ensuring your profile enhances rather than detracts from your candidacy, as a weak GitHub becomes an anti-signal that hurts your chances
