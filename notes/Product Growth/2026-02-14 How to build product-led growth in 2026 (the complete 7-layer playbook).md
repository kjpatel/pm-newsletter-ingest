# How to build product-led growth in 2026 (the complete 7-layer playbook)

**Source**: [Product Growth](https://www.news.aakashg.com/p/plg-in-2026)
**Author**: Aakash Gupta | **Date**: Feb 14, 2026

---

## Summary

The traditional 2018 PLG playbook (free trials, viral loops, activation focus) is obsolete; successful 2026 PLG requires a comprehensive 7-layer framework focusing on go-to-market, information architecture, conversion, activation, retention, monetization, and expansion—with real examples from Canva, Figma, and Attio showing massive competitive advantages.

## Key Takeaways

- **Shift from traditional to product-led channels**: Move marketing focus from brand advertising (banners, billboards) to product-led SEO/AEO/GEO strategies that drive organic discovery with zero friction entry points like Canva's "build an Instagram post" landing pages.
- **Personalize website experiences by user type**: Design longer, segmented homepage journeys that immediately identify user personas (Workplace, Educator, Creator) and guide them toward relevant pricing tiers rather than one-size-fits-all CTAs.
- **Secure leadership buy-in across three pillars**: PLG success requires CEO alignment, VP of Sales support, and VP of Marketing buy-in for PLG lead generation—tactical execution alone fails without organizational strategy and people dedicated to rapid experimentation.
- **Focus on your most broken layer first**: Diagnose which of the 7 layers (go-to-market, information, conversion, activation, retention, monetization, expansion) is your primary constraint, then optimize that layer plus 1-2 layers below it for maximum impact.
