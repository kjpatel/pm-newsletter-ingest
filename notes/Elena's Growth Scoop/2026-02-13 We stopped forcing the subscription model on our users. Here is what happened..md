# We stopped forcing the subscription model on our users. Here is what happened.

**Source**: [Elena's Growth Scoop](https://www.elenaverna.com/p/we-stopped-forcing-the-subscription)
**Author**: Elena Verna | **Date**: Feb 13, 2026

---

## Summary

Not every business should force a subscription model on users; many AI companies, including Lovable, are discovering that alternative monetization strategies better serve their customers and business goals.

## Key Takeaways

- **Rethink your monetization model** - Challenge the default assumption that subscriptions are right for your product; consider whether your customers actually want recurring billing or if it creates friction.
- **Listen to customer resistance** - If users are pushing back against your subscription model, it's a signal to evaluate alternative pricing strategies like pay-as-you-go, tiered usage, or one-time purchases.
- **Align monetization with product value** - Choose pricing models that match how customers actually derive value from your product rather than forcing a model that optimizes for investor preferences (ARR).
- **AI products need different economics** - AI companies in particular are recognizing that subscription models may not align with variable usage costs and customer consumption patterns.
