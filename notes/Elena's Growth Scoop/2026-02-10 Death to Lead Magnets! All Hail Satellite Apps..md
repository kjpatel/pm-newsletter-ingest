# Death to Lead Magnets! All Hail Satellite Apps.

**Source**: [Elena's Growth Scoop](https://www.elenaverna.com/p/death-to-lead-magnets-all-hail-satellite)
**Author**: Elena Verna (featuring Jonathan) | **Date**: Feb 10, 2026

---

## Summary

Lead magnets are becoming obsolete as a growth strategy; instead, companies should build lightweight 'satellite apps' that provide immediate value and seamlessly convert users into paying customers. This shift represents a fundamental change in how B2B SaaS companies should approach customer acquisition and onboarding.

## Key Takeaways

- **Replace lead magnets with satellite apps** that deliver real functionality rather than gated content, reducing friction in the user journey from discovery to conversion.
- **Design for immediate value delivery** by creating lightweight, focused applications that solve a specific problem and naturally lead users toward your core product.
- **Optimize the conversion path** by embedding upgrade pathways directly into satellite apps so users experience the core product's benefits before committing to a purchase.
- **Reduce friction in acquisition** by eliminating email walls and signup requirements upfront, allowing users to experience your product ecosystem organically.
