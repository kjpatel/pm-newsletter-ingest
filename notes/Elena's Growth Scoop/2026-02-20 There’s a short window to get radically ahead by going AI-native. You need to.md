# There’s a short window to get radically ahead by going AI-native. You need to act now.

**Source**: [Elena's Growth Scoop](https://www.elenaverna.com/p/theres-a-short-window-to-get-radically)
**Author**: Elena Verna | **Date**: Feb 20, 2026

---

## Summary

The article argues that while AI disruption is inevitable and jobs will be displaced, the real opportunity lies in a narrow window where individuals who become truly AI-native can gain an unfair competitive advantage—far beyond simply being aware of AI tools.

## Key Takeaways

- **Shift your mindset** from defensive (AI will take my job) to offensive (AI is my biggest career opportunity) to capitalize on the current advantage window.
- **Go AI-native, not AI-aware** by deeply integrating AI into your daily workflows and decision-making, not just using it occasionally.
- **Act urgently** because the window for getting radically ahead is short and will close as AI adoption becomes normalized across industries.
- **Study Matt Shumer's thesis** on accelerating AI progress to understand the magnitude of disruption coming and why adaptation speed matters now.
