# How to use AI for your next job interview

**Source**: [Lenny's Newsletter](https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/p/how-to-use-ai-for-your-next-job-interview)
**Author**: Lenny Rachitsky (featuring Noam Segal) | **Date**: Feb 24, 2026

---

## Summary

Noam Segal reveals how tech professionals use AI to improve interview performance and introduces a free Claude Code-based interview coach built from insights gathered from 30+ candidates.

## Key Takeaways

- **Use AI for feedback loops** - Leverage AI interview coaches to get line-by-line feedback on your answers and identify blind spots traditional prep methods miss.
- **Predict common questions** - Build AI systems that surface likely interview questions specific to your target role before you walk in the door.
- **Surface authentic stories** - Use AI to uncover relevant professional stories and experiences you can authentically share rather than sounding like every other AI-prepped candidate.
- **Implement action checklists** - Follow structured before, during, and after interview actions to systematically improve your performance across multiple interviews.
