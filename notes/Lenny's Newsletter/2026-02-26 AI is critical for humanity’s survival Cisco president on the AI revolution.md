## Summary

Jeetu Patel, Cisco's president and CPO, discusses how AI has become critical for organizational survival and shares his six-part framework for building great companies along with communication strategies for scaling leadership across large organizations.

## Key Takeaways

- **Apply the six-part framework** to evaluate your company's strength across timing, market, team, product, brand, and distribution—each element is essential for sustainable success.
- **Implement the 'right to win' strategic framework** to clearly define where your organization has competitive advantages and concentrate resources there rather than competing everywhere.
- **Reverse your communication approach** by praising privately and criticizing publicly (opposite of conventional wisdom) to drive accountability and foster a culture of continuous improvement.
- **Embed AI-first thinking** across all organizational layers, not just product teams, to remain competitive as AI becomes critical infrastructure for business survival.
- **Design communication systems to prevent 'packet loss'** by creating clear channels and redundancy in how information flows across large organizations to prevent critical messages from being lost.
