## Summary

This Community Wisdom edition curates practical insights from Lenny's Slack community on navigating product challenges including enterprise readiness, aligning engineering and product, managing PRD evolution, and work planning strategies.

## Key Takeaways

- **Make enterprise readiness a strategic priority** by planning infrastructure, compliance, and scalability requirements early rather than retrofitting them after product-market fit
- **Align engineering and product cadences** by establishing clear communication channels and shared metrics when engineering velocity outpaces product strategy decisions
- **Version PRDs dynamically** as features evolve by treating documentation as a living artifact that reflects current product state rather than a static artifact
- **Plan work around team energy levels** by identifying high-energy periods for complex work and low-energy periods for maintenance tasks to optimize productivity
