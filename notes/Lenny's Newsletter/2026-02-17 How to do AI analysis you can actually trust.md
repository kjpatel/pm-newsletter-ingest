# How to do AI analysis you can actually trust

**Source**: [Lenny's Newsletter](https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/p/how-to-do-ai-analysis-you-can-actually-db6)
**Author**: Lenny Rachitsky (featuring Caitlin Sullivan) | **Date**: Feb 17, 2026

## Summary

Caitlin Sullivan shares four proven prompting techniques for obtaining trustworthy, actionable insights from LLMs when analyzing customer data, drawing from 2,000+ hours of testing to identify and fix common AI analysis failure modes.

## Key Takeaways

- **Implement verification passes** that stress-test AI analysis results before presenting them in decks to catch fabricated data and hallucinations.
- **Use specific prompting techniques** to prevent AI from defaulting to generic themes and insights when analyzing customer research data.
- **Know which LLMs perform best** for analysis work—different models have varying reliability levels, with some prone to fabrication.
- **Transform vague signals into clarity** by applying structured prompting methods that help convert ambiguous customer insights into actionable decisions.
