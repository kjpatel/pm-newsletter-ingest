# 🎙️ This week on How I AI: Mastering Midjourney: How to create consistent, beautiful brand imagery without complex prompts

**Source**: [Lenny's Newsletter](https://www.lennysnewsletter.com/p/this-week-on-how-i-ai-mastering-midjourney)
**Author**: Lenny Rachitsky (featuring Jamey Gannon) | **Date**: Mar 09, 2026

---

## Summary

AI creative director Jamey Gannon shares her practical system for generating consistent, high-quality brand imagery in Midjourney by minimizing text prompts and leveraging style references, image references, and personalization codes instead.

## Key Takeaways

- **Minimize prompting** by relying on visual references (style references, image references, and personalization codes) rather than writing complex text descriptions to communicate with Midjourney.
- **Use style references over mood boards** because SREFs provide direct style guidance while varied mood boards cause Midjourney to average out styles, resulting in less consistent outputs.
- **Reference known publications or camera models** as shorthand for complex aesthetics—saying "Dazed editorial" instantly communicates lighting, composition, and style without lengthy descriptions.
- **Remove problematic elements entirely** instead of trying to fix them through prompting; if references are introducing unwanted styles or colors, crop or delete them completely.
- **Understand how the AI perceives your inputs** rather than forcing it to work your way; this perspective shift helps troubleshoot issues and leads to better results.
