## Summary

Jason Del Rey discusses his transition to independent media with The Aisle, covering AI and commerce, reflecting on his journalism career, influences from other indie creators, and his evolving approach to balancing breaking news with in-depth reporting.

## Key Takeaways

- **Build trust directly with subscribers** – Jason emphasizes that independent media creators have earned enough credibility through their track record to deliver their best judgment on content, evidenced by his 60%+ open rates on 1200-1800 word pieces.
- **Balance news cycles with deeper analysis** – The 'snacks vs. meals' tension remains real, but independent writers should lean toward longer-form content when it serves their audience rather than defaulting to breaking news.
- **Study multi-platform indie creators** – Jason names specific playbook models (Casey Newton's prolificness, Alex Kantrowitz's hustle, multi-media approaches mixing newsletter/pods/events) worth emulating for indie success.
- **Don't underestimate paradigm shifts** – Jason admits missing the full scope of ChatGPT in 2023 due to other priorities, a cautionary tale about staying tuned to transformative moments in your coverage area.
- **Recognize private vs. public behavior gaps** – Many tech industry figures publicly criticize media but engage privately with fair, well-researched reporting, so don't assume hostile public positioning means unreachable sources.
