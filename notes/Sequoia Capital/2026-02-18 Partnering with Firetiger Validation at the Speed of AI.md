# Partnering with Firetiger: Validation at the Speed of AI

## Summary

Firetiger is a new AI-powered observability platform that uses autonomous agents to monitor systems 24/7, detect anomalies, and fix issues before customers notice, solving the challenge of keeping up with rapidly shipped AI-generated code.

## Key Takeaways

- **Deploy autonomous monitoring agents** that work continuously to detect and resolve issues in real-time, reducing the manual burden on engineering teams to manage traditional dashboards and incident response.
- **Leverage AI agents as watchers** to observe other AI agents, creating a scalable observability solution that matches the pace of AI-native development where code generation happens at unprecedented speeds.
- **Prioritize customer impact detection** by building observability that identifies which anomalies matter, who they affect, and fixes them proactively rather than reactively alerting engineers after problems occur.
