## Summary

Ben Thompson critiques viral AI doomer articles for lacking appreciation of market dynamics, while explaining why DoorDash will benefit from AI advantages despite concerns about disruption.

## Key Takeaways

- **Reject static analysis**: AI doomer narratives fail because they don't account for how markets and companies dynamically adapt to technological change.
- **Focus on competitive positioning**: Companies with existing operational advantages (like DoorDash's logistics infrastructure) are better positioned to capture AI value than startups.
- **Understand market resilience**: Historical precedent shows that industries disrupted by technology often see winners emerge rather than total collapse.
