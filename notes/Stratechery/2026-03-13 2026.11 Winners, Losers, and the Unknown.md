## Summary

This week's Stratechery bundle highlights key developments in AI integration, tech earnings, and entertainment, with particular focus on how model makers themselves are best positioned to harness AI capabilities through platforms like Copilot Cowork, challenging earlier assumptions about AI commoditization.

## Key Takeaways

- **Prioritize integration over commodities**: Model makers like Anthropic are winning by controlling the integration layer around AI capabilities, suggesting that bundled solutions matter more than isolated models.
- **Watch infrastructure investments**: Microsoft's strategy shift from betting on OpenAI integration to building infrastructure around models indicates where competitive advantages are consolidating in the AI value chain.
- **Monitor geopolitical tech implications**: Recent Iran war developments have reshaped U.S.-China dynamics and influenced tech policy decisions across multiple sectors.
- **Evaluate cloud-first architecture**: Apple's MacBook Neo success demonstrates that strong hardware combined with cloud-based software can deliver value at lower price points, changing traditional hardware economics.
