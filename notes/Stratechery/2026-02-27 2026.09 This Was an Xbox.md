# 2026.09: This Was an Xbox

**Source**: [Stratechery](https://stratechery.com/2026/this-was-an-xbox/)
**Author**: Ben Thompson | **Date**: Feb 27, 2026

---

## Summary

Microsoft's Xbox division is likely exiting the first-party hardware business due to stagnant gaming market growth and competitive pressures elsewhere in the company, marking the end of a 15-year strategy that proved misaligned with market realities.

## Key Takeaways

- **Recognize strategic sunk costs**: Microsoft held onto Xbox primarily as evidence of consumer appeal rather than for financial performance, a cautionary tale about letting identity drive business decisions.
- **Pivot when market dynamics shift**: With gaming market growth stalled and competitive pressures mounting on Microsoft's core business, hardware-focused gaming strategies may no longer be viable.
- **Acknowledge historical misalignment**: Xbox's strategy has been misaligned for at least 15 years; recognizing these red flags earlier could have saved resources and accelerated necessary pivots.
