# 2026.08: Losing in the Attention Economy

**Source**: [Stratechery](https://stratechery.com/2026/losing-in-the-attention-economy/)
**Author**: Ben Thompson (featuring Matthew Ball) | **Date**: Feb 20, 2026

---

## Summary

Industries built on innovation and differentiation face existential challenges when forced to compete for attention in saturated markets, as exemplified by video games losing relevance despite technological superiority and structural advantages.

## Key Takeaways

- **Recognize attention scarcity** — In competitive markets, fighting for user attention can expose competitive weaknesses; being technically superior or innovative doesn't guarantee market dominance when attention is the limiting resource.
- **Understand structural shifts** — Industries optimized for previous paradigms (like thick clients in gaming) may find their advantages become liabilities when market dynamics fundamentally change; adapt business models accordingly.
- **Monitor industry health signals** — Watch for signs of sector-wide problems beyond individual company performance, such as flagging engagement, audience apathy, or oversaturation, which indicate deeper systemic issues requiring strategic repositioning.
